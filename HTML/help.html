Help & FAQ

How do I register for an event?

  • Go to the Register for an Upcoming Event section.
  • Fill in your name, email, date, and select your preferred event type.
  • Click Register. You’ll see a confirmation message if successful.

How do I give feedback?

  • Scroll to the Event Feedback section.
  • Enter your phone number, select the event, and type your feedback.
  • Click Submit Feedback to send your comments.

How do I watch the event promo video?

  • Find the Watch Our Event Promo! section.
  • Click the play button on the video. When it’s ready, a message will appear.

How do I find nearby events?

  • Click the Find Nearby Events button.
  • Allow location access in your browser to see your coordinates.

How do I contact the organizers?

  • Go to the Contact section at the bottom of the page.
  • Use the provided email link to send your questions: selvamarshall13@gmail.com

Troubleshooting

  • If you see errors or something doesn’t work, try refreshing the page.
  • Make sure you have allowed location access for geolocation features.
  • For further help, contact us by email.
